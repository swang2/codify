Sushi to Die For

The South Bay’s Best Spot To Get RAW

Step Into Your Senses






Blowfish Sushi provides an exciting and unconventional environment that is hip, unique and inviting. The Restaurant’s modern Tokyo design features innovative architectural and design elements. The atmosphere is stimulated by upbeat ambient music experienced through a state of the art sound system, Japanese Animation streaming across multiple video screens and a collection of original Manga production cells.

Blowfish Sushi is an eclectic sushi bar and restaurant that serves both modern and traditional sushi, sashimi, as well as sakes, cooked appetizers, specialty desserts and offers a wide variety of catering services. The Asian Fusion menu style emphasizes clean, pure, healthy foods. Premium imported sakes and an original line of sake cocktails are featured in the restaurant’s stunning liquor lounge. ”Walk into Blowfish Sushi and step into your senses; its decor houses a modern menu, reinterprets sushi and presents a new palette for the genre”
